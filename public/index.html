About Me

Before Code Fellows I was an explorer of learning opportunities. If I found something interesting, I had to learn more. This led to many adventures both near and far, from massage therapy, health education, writing and crafting. In my last job, I mainly worked in design and basic front-end. I found that I very much enjoyed the more technical aspects such as building web stores for clients and designing merchandize for these stores. Encouragement from my social circle, as well as suggestions for resources such as Code Academy, led me to explore the coding bootcamp path. I enjoy making things and I think this benefits me as a Software Developer as I can bring creativity and discipline to the process. My varied job history and experiences help bring a fresh perspective to the field.
